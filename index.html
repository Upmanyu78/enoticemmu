Connecting admins, faculty, and students

Welcome to Information Multicast System (IMS), your go-to platform for seamless communication in the educational sector. Based in Ambala, IMS features three integrated modules: Admin, Faculty, and Students. Admins can efficiently manage faculty and student data, while faculty members can post important notices directly to student dashboards. With all areas of your institution connected, enhancing the overall learning experience. Join us in transforming educational communication.
